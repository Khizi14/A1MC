Critique Close zocdoc Today I will talk about Zocdoc, an application which is being used to book appointments with doctors and then go to there office for the appointments.

Pros

  • ZocDoc puts together a list of participating doctors that are near your location-wise.

  • Another neat feature is that ZocDoc saves your profile and all of your past booking information.

  • End-to-End Encryption.

  • Patients can book appointments at all hours of the day.

  • The main benefit for doctors to use ZocDoc is that it is a form of marketing to help bring in new patients.

  • Shows Doctors profile.

  • Dose check insurance before appointment booking.

Cons

  • No Chat option

  • Appointment timings are not matched with what is shown.

  • Appointments being double booked which creates mess.

  • Do not have wide range of doctors

  • Can Be Hacked

  • No Sign-out

The Idea is to provide people services through chat and provide people a way to save their earnings rather than taking money from them for every action on application just to later find the appointment was cancelled.
